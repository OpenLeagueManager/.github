# Open League Manager

Esports team management for League of Legends — lead your team from regional leagues to world championships.

Free and open-source, built by the community for the community.

## What is it?

Open League Manager is a management game where you take charge of a professional League of Legends team: transfers, tactics, training and financial management, all with the goal of turning your club into a champion.

## Key features

- Roster and contract management
- Training scheduling and match preparation
- Seasons, tournaments and progression ~~to international competitions~~ (soon?)
- Match simulation and detailed statistics
